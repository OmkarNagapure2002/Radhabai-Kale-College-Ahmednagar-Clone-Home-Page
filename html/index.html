Our Moto

"Education through self-help" is our motto

Our Vission

Women empowerment through self-sufficing, self-reliant and self-respecting education and to visualize the institute as the best center for development and application of knowledge contributing to the modern nation.

Our Mission

  1. To impart and evolve quality higher education for women to make them academically and emotionally competent and socio- economically elf-reliant.
  2. To inculcate values and to promote women’s education

Our Objective

  1. To promote women's education.
  2. To develop the overall personality of the women students.
  3. To impart education through self-help and dignity of labour.
